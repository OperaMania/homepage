Tuition in Dresden

Tomomi has an established private teaching practice in Dresden and offers lessons on the FLUTE, BAROQUE FLUTE, RECORDER, PIANO and CLAVICHORD.

 

She also teaches:
MUSIC THEORY, IMPROVISING, ORNAMENTAION on early music
JAPANESE and JAPANESE CULTURES (Calligraphy, Abacus, etc...)

 

She also has a qualification for ensemble and choir conducting. If you would need an ensemble or a choir conductor for your group she could help you.

 

You can choose to take part in group or individual lessons, a friendly, nurturing environment will stimulate you to learn to play instruments and help you enjoy the process of making music, every step of the way - I teach instruments at all ages and levels from beginners to advanced.

  • 1998 - 2002 Study of flute and music pedagogical study of music teacher for high school at the Osaka University of Arts, Japan
  • 2003 - 2005 Pedagogical study at the Bukkyo University in Kyoto, Japan, teaching diploma for primary school
  • 2005 - 2007 Several postgratuate courses in music, study at the Berufsfachschule für Musk in Dinkelsbühl, Germany, main subject: Flute, baroque flute, qualification for ensemble and choir conducting
  • 2007 - 2009 Study of the baroque flute at the Konservatorium in Vienna, Austria

  • since 2008  Baroque flute lesson with Lisa Beznosiuk, Nancy Hadden in London, Boris Kapfer in Lyon
